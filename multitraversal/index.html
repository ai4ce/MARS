Multitraversal

3-month span, 66 locations, 1.4 million frames.

Among the 66 locations, 48 have over 20 traversals, 23 over 100 traversals, and 6 over 200 traversals. Each traversal has 250 frames (25 seconds) on average, with the majority of traversals containing 100 to 400 frames (10 to 40 seconds).

Note: the 67 locations are indexed from 0-66, but #2 has been removed due to scarcity of data.
